Goa Medical Council, a Statutory Body constituted in July 1993, as a result of enactment of Goa Medical Council Act, 1991 by the Government of Goa. Goa Medical Council is vested with powers, duties and function of regulating the practice of Modern Scientific System of Medicine in the State of Goa.
